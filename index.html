Southwest Virginia Is More Than People Think

From the New River Valley to the Blue Ridge to the Virginia Highlands — Southwest Virginia is a region of extraordinary character, independent communities, and people who are building something worth paying attention to.

The Region

Southwest Virginia doesn't get the attention it deserves. The national conversation moves fast and tends to overlook places that aren't major metros. But the communities here — from Pulaski to Galax to Bluefield — have stories worth telling and people worth knowing.

LinksSWVA is the broadest view in the LINKS community network — a regional lens covering the New River Valley and the surrounding counties that form the broader Southwest Virginia landscape. Same values as LinksPulaski and LinksNRV, wider geographic scope.

The region has genuine assets: mountain landscapes, strong agricultural roots, manufacturing heritage, growing tech presence through Virginia Tech, and communities that have learned how to do more with less. That story deserves to be told honestly and well.

Written from Pulaski, Virginia — at the center of it all.
